Commercial Support and Services

The following companies offer additional services for FreeRADIUS. A listing here does not mean that the FreeRADIUS project endorses these companies. It simply means that they are on record as providing services for FreeRADIUS.

Business listings (in alphabetical order)

  • Authenter

    Authentor is a consultancy, based in the UK, that specialises in network authentication. FreeRADIUS is Authenter's preferred RADIUS server. Authenter has implemented FreeRADIUS within VPN (PPTP), wireless (802.1X), secure web-server (Apache) and Slipstream deployments.

  • Cladju Consulting, Inc.

    FreeRADIUS-specific training, development, and technical support.

  • Suntel Communications

    Based in Istanbul, Turkey we have FreeRADIUS deployments for large corporates, banks, telcos and ISPs throughout the region.

  • Switch Management

    Switch Management provides RADIUS ASP -- a fault-tolerant, hosted RADIUS solution for VoIP providers that uses FreeRADIUS, as well as WebCDR -- a Web-based billing system for wholesale VoIP carriers that mediates FreeRADIUS log files

  • TRI-D Systems, Inc.

    We provide installation and technical support for FreeRADIUS-based One-Time Password (OTP) deployments, including desktop login, VPN login (PPTP, IPSec, and OpenVPN) and service-specific login (IMAP, WWW, etc.).

  • Xelerance Corporation

    Xelerence Corporation is a small team of experienced software developers, network designers and consultants who are providing support, development and network design services for secure, Linux based systems. This includes IPsec (Openswan), DNSSEC, and Freeradius. We also provide clustering systems.
