Support and Services

The following companies offer additional services for FreeRADIUS. A listing here does not mean that the FreeRADIUS project endorses these companies. It simply means that they are on record as providing services for FreeRADIUS.

FreeRADIUS is also actively looking for sponsors for future projects. These projects include support for WiMAX, 3GPP2, and Change of Authorization (CoA).

Business listings (in alphabetical order)

  • Authenter

    Authentor is a consultancy, based in the UK, that specialises in network authentication. FreeRADIUS is Authenter's preferred RADIUS server. Authenter has implemented FreeRADIUS within VPN (PPTP), wireless (802.1X), secure web-server (Apache) and Slipstream deployments.

  • Network RADIUS Inc..

    Network RADIUS was founded by some of the core FreeRADIUS developers to provide training, support, and custom development for FreeRADIUS. Our customers include multiple Fortune 50 companies.

  • WebCDR

    WebCDR provides WebCDR RADIUS -- a fault-tolerant, hosted RADIUS solution for VoIP providers that uses FreeRADIUS, as well as WebCDR Billing -- a Web-based billing system for wholesale VoIP carriers that mediates FreeRADIUS log files, and WebCDR Watchdog -- a Web-based monitoring tool for wholesale VoIP carriers that sends QOS and fraud alerts based on FreeRADIUS data.
