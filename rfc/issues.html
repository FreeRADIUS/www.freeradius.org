Open Issues with Standards

This page lists the open issues with standards related to FreeRADIUS

Acct-Status-Type and subsystems

The Acct-Status-Type attribute has only a few values defined. These values do not fully match new practices in RADIUS. The Acct-Status-Type subsystem page discusses the problems and recommended solutions.

Acct-Session-Id and Acct-Multi-Session-Id uniqueness

The Acct-Session-Id and Acct-Multi-Session-Id attributes are opaque tokens generated by the NAS. They are not required to be unique. The Acct-Session-Id uniqueness page discusses the problems and recommended solutions.
