Operating Systems

The server builds and runs on a wide variety of operating systems. Instructions for installing it on a wide variety of systems are available on the Wiki build page.

The following operating systems are supported by FreeRADIUS.

  • Linux (Ubuntu, Debian, Suse, Redhat, etc.)
  • FreeBSD
  • MAC OSX
  • NetBSD
  • OpenBSD
  • DragonFlyBSD
  • Solaris
  • Cygwin
  • Native Windows (in planning stages via MingW)
  • HPUX (not recently tested)
  • AIX (not recently tested)
  • OSF (not recently tested)
