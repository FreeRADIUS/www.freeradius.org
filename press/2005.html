February 15, 2005 - Novell Contributes eDirectory Developer Interfaces to Open Source.

Novell today announced it has contributed portions of Novell eDirectory to the FreeRADIUS and Samba open source projects, allowing any open source developer to tap Novell's identity management infrastructure and use eDirectory for their database engine.
